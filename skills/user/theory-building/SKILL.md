### Capability 4: Bias Detection & Mitigation

**Continuously Monitor and Flag:**

**1. Confirmation Bias**
- **Detection:** Only positive evidence cited
- **Alert:** "üö® You cite 5 supporting studies but no contradictory findings"
- **Action:** "Search: [your constructs] AND (contradict* OR fail*)"

**2. Availability Heuristic**
- **Detection:** Reliance on recent/salient cases
- **Alert:** "üö® Your 3 examples are all from tech industry"
- **Action:** "Suggest systematic sampling across industries"

**3. Anchoring**
- **Detection:** Stuck on initial theoretical lens
- **Alert:** "üö® You started with RBV, still framing everything as resources"
- **Action:** "Restart fresh from institutional theory perspective"

**4. HARKing (Hypothesizing After Results Known)**
- **Detection:** Hypothesis changes after results
- **Alert:** "üö® Your hypothesis differs from initial statement"
- **Action:** "Distinguish clearly: a priori vs post-hoc hypotheses"

**5. Theory-Induced Blindness**
- **Detection:** Ignoring data inconsistent with theory
- **Alert:** "üö® Case K contradicts your theory but you dismissed it"
- **Action:** "What if Case K is the key? Explore this anomaly"

**6. Narrative Fallacy**
- **Detection:** Post-hoc storytelling without mechanism
- **Alert:** "üö® You describe 'what' happened but not 'how'"
- **Action:** "Specify the causal mechanism step-by-step"

**When Bias Detected:**

3. Suggest specific mitigation
4. Provide better practice example

- Key constructs: Asset specificity, uncertainty, frequency
- Boundary: Economic efficiency primary concern

- Core: Isomorphism for legitimacy
- Key constructs: Coercive, mimetic, normative pressures
- Boundary: Organizations care about legitimacy

**Dynamic Capabilities**
- Core: Sense, seize, reconfigure resources
- Key constructs: Absorptive capacity, recombination
- Boundary: Dynamic environments, need for adaptation

**Social Network Theory**
- Core: Network position determines outcomes
- Key constructs: Centrality, structural holes, tie strength
- Boundary: Relational resources matter
